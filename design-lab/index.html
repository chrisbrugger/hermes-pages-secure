03 · Noir DossierBest for investigations, competitor dossiers, strategic memos
EYES ONLY

The quiet competitor is moving upstream.

A document-forward style that makes the report feel authored, deliberate, and serious without resorting to cyberpunk neon.

01 / FINDING

Hiring patterns and enterprise messaging changed before the public roadmap did. Treat the repositioning as intentional, not exploratory.

02 / EVIDENCE

Three sales pages now lead with governance, migration safety, and auditability. Pricing pages still lag, which suggests the motion is being tested with larger accounts first.

03 / RESPONSE

Do not mirror their narrative. Publish proof of deployment speed and operational independence; force the comparison back to time-to-value.

Source reliability

“The useful part of the story is not what changed. It is what they stopped saying.”
